Experience Our Advanced Dental Technologies

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we're committed to staying at the forefront of dental technology to provide our patients with the best possible care.

CAD/CAM Technology

Our CAD/CAM system enables us to create precise and custom dental restorations, such as crowns, bridges, and veneers, in a single visit. This technology ensures a perfect fit and natural appearance.

Digital X-Rays

Our state-of-the-art digital X-ray system provides high-resolution images with significantly less radiation exposure compared to traditional X-rays.

We have incorporated advanced laser technology into our practice, which allows for minimally invasive procedures with reduced pain and faster healing times.

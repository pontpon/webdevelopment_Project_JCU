Our New Dental Technologies

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we are committed to staying at the forefront of dental technology to provide our patients with the best possible care.

Digital X-Rays

High-resolution images with significantly less radiation exposure compared to traditional X-rays.

Intraoral Cameras

Detailed view of your mouth with high-quality images of teeth and gums for better diagnosis.

Laser Dentistry

Advanced laser technology for minimally invasive procedures with reduced pain and faster healing.

CAD/CAM Technology

Create precise and custom dental restorations in a single visit with perfect fit and natural appearance.

3D Printing

Create accurate dental models, surgical guides, and custom appliances for precise treatment planning.

Teledentistry

Virtual consultations and dental care from the comfort of your home with professional advice.

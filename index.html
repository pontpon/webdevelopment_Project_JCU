Welcome to 100SMILES

A modern dental care facility located at 33 Fantail St. in Razole for over three decades, our roots in the community run deep. Our unwavering dedication is to provide the best possible dental care for our patients. With over 50 years of experience in dental care, we pride ourselves on offering friendly, reliable, and compassionate services.

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we understand the diverse needs of our clients, and that's why we offer a comprehensive range of dental services. From routine wellness check-ups and cleanings to dental implants, orthodontics, and cosmetic dentistry, we provide a full spectrum of preventive and restorative care to keep your smile healthy and beautiful.
